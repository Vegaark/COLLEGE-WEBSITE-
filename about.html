About Us

Learn about our history, vision and mission

Our Story

Meridian College has been a center of learning for over 60 years, offering quality education across Arts, Science, Commerce and IT. Our goal is to build not just successful careers, but confident and responsible individuals.


Our Vision

To be a respected center of learning where academic excellence and good character grow side by side.


Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality education supported by experienced faculty, modern facilities and a caring campus community.

Principal's Message

Dr. Ruby carnor

"We do not measure a graduating class by its placement statistics alone, but by the clarity of thought and steadiness of character each student carries out of these gates."
